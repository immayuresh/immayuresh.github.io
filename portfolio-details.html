IOT Based Auto Feeding Robot (Daycare Bot)

This Project was to design a Pet daycare robot that can monitor as well as feed the dogs or cats in a timely manner.

  • Rating:
  • Project: Final Year Project
  • Completed: 3 may 2022

People love their pets and vice versa, but there are times you need to leave your pets at home for long durations alone and this is a problematic issue. Well, we here propose to design a doggy day care robot that can monitor as well as feed the dogs or cats in a timely manner

The following are the primary characteristics of the Raspberry Pi-powered Day Care Robot:

  • 1. IoT-based remote monitoring of your pets
  • 2. Using voice commands to communicate with pets
  • 3. Food and Water Containers for Dogs and Cats
  • 4. Feeding of cat/dog food and water on a regular basis using a feeding tray
  • 5. Plastic Mesh protects the robot's main modules from naughty pets.
    • The Robot is an IoT-based robot that can look after your pets while you are away from home. The robot is equipped with a camera that enables for live broadcasting of home footage through an IoT network. The robot goes far beyond a security camera in that you can control it remotely over the internet and move it about your property whenever you want.
    This allows you to be with your dogs regardless of your location. The robot also has a speaker that speaks to your dogs/cats, allowing you to yell at them for misbehaving or remind them when it's time to feed them. . The robot is a 3 wheeled drive system with a feeding tray and Plastic Bin 1 Stores Dog Food/Cat Food.
    The robot pours the correct amount of food and water in the feeding tray, as directed by the user online, and then glides open the feeding tray, as well as calling out your dog or cat by name to alert you to feeding time. When the pet has finished eating, the food tray is closed. The pet owner can keep track of everything online. This complete system is run by a Raspberry Pi controller, which enables efficient control of all robot functions

    Pick and Place Robotic Arm

    This Project was to design and develop the Pick and Place Robotic Arm based on Arduino Uno for minimizing human error and to make the work faster and efficient.

    • Rating:
    • Project: Third Year Project
    • Completed: 9 may 2021

    Objectives:

  • 1. To create the robotic arm model and develop it.
  • 2. Selecting and organising the parts is part of designing this robot.
  • 3. Through this project, we have gained knowledge about embedded electronics and robotics.

    • The important objectives that are associated in installing of robotic system in the industry are:
  • 1. Saving human resources.
  • 2. Better performance and effectiveness.
  • 3. The capacity to function under adverse circumstances.
    • A Robot is a virtually intelligent agent capable of carrying out tasks robotically with the help of some supervision. Practically, a robot is basically an electro-mechanical machine that is guided by means of computer and electronic programming.
    Robotic applications are widely use in research laboratories and industries to automate processes and reduce human errors.
    Some of the tasks perform by robots include assembly lines and motion that require force control with feedback to its controller.
    Purpose of work is to minimize the human errors, make the work more faster and efficient.
    A Robot is a reprogrammable, multifunctional manipulator designed to move material, parts or devices through various programmed motions.
